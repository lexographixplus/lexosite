Our Platforms

Dedicated spaces to support, showcase, and connect Gambian writers and their work. Explore what we've built for our literary community.

LexoGraphix Plus has created these platforms to empower authors, facilitate learning, and provide access to compelling Gambian literature.

Knowledge Base

A dynamic educational hub offering self-publishing guidance, tips, and best practices for aspiring and established authors.

Key Benefits:

  • Empowers writers with step-by-step information on writing, formatting, publishing, and promotion.
  • Accessible and regularly updated content tailored to Gambian and African self-publishing needs.
  • Supports discovery through search, filters, and categorized topics.
  • Builds authority for LexoGraphix Plus as a thought leader in digital publishing.
Explore Knowledge Base

Author Directory

A digital showcase of writers affiliated with or supported by LexoGraphix Plus, offering visibility and networking opportunities.

Key Benefits:

  • Promotes emerging and established Gambian authors by highlighting their bios, genres, and works.
  • Enables readers and collaborators to discover new talent and connect with writers.
  • Encourages community-building and partnerships among authors.
  • Provides a platform for authors to link their publications and grow their personal brands.
View Author Directory

Book Store

An online storefront to explore, preview, and access books published or promoted by LexoGraphix Plus.

Key Benefits:

  • Centralized access to all books published under or promoted by LexoGraphix Plus.
  • Offers free and paid downloads, previews, and promotional banners to increase engagement and sales.
  • Boosts visibility for authors and encourages local book readership.
  • Supports sustainable monetization for self-published authors through various channels.
