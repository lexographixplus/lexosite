Our Services

Tailored solutions for young Gambian writers to publish, promote, and succeed. We're here to support your literary journey every step of the way.

LexoGraphix Plus offers a suite of services specifically designed to help young Gambian writers bring their literary creations to life and connect with readers.

Partnership Publishing for Young Writers

(Royalty-Based Model)

For young authors seeking a collaborative and supportive publishing experience with a focus on professional presentation and reaching their target audience within The Gambia and beyond.

LexoGraphix Plus Provides:

  • Support and Guidance: Tailored throughout the publishing process, addressing unique challenges young Gambian writers face.
  • Professional Cover Design: Eye-catching and culturally relevant book covers (if not already provided by the author).
  • Interior Layout and Formatting: Professional formatting for both print and digital platforms.
  • Printing Coordination: Collaborating with trusted local printers for quality and affordability.
  • Targeted Book Promotion: Online listings, social media campaigns, promotional materials, and outreach to relevant organizations.
  • Local Distribution Assistance: Support in getting books into bookstores and community networks.

Young Author Responsibilities:

  • Commitment to Editing: Ensuring manuscripts are thoroughly edited.
  • Active Promotion Participation: Engaging in marketing efforts and leveraging personal networks.

Book Promotion Services for Authors

(For Already Published Books)

For young authors who have already published their books and need assistance in increasing visibility and reaching a wider readership in The Gambia.

Services Offered (Tailored to Author Needs):

  • Social Media Marketing Campaigns: Targeted campaigns on platforms popular with young Gambians.
  • Online Engagement Strategies: Training on how to interact with readers online and build a following.
  • Outreach to Schools and Youth Groups: Facilitating connections for book talks and signings.
  • Local Literary Event Participation: Assistance in attending relevant literary events and workshops.
Boost Your Book's Reach

Self-Publishing Consultancy for Young Writers

For authors who want to self-publish but need guidance tailored to their experience and local resources in The Gambia.

Services Offered (À la Carte or Package):

  • Simplified Publishing Roadmap: Step-by-step guidance for self-publishing in The Gambia.
  • Affordable Editing & Proofreading Resources: Advice on finding local editing support.
  • Culturally Relevant Cover Design & Layout Consultation: Ensuring books appeal to Gambian audiences.
  • ISBN and Basic Publishing Requirements: Guidance on obtaining ISBNs and meeting standards.
  • Online & Local Distribution Advice: Helping authors understand where and how to sell their books.
  • Marketing & Promotion Strategies: Teaching authors how to promote themselves and their work.
